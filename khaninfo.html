Abdul Ghaffar Khan

  • Khan was born in Utmanzai, India(now Pakistan) in 1890
  • He was born to rich landowners
  • He was against the partition, and chose to live in Pakistan
  • He believed in and fought for: Indian Independence, Hindu-Muslim Unity, and Pashtun(a minority of Muslims) rights
  • He was a friend and asscosiate to Mohandas K. Gandhi
  • He created a school in 1910 when he was only 20, it was later banned by the British in 1915
  • When he became friends with Gandhi it influenced him to join politics. Hence in 1929 he attended the Indian National Congress meeting, and shortly after founded the Red Shirt Movement, a movement fighting for Indian Independence non-violently
